Voxel Frontier

Mine the land,
drop the loot, build your path.

This is a single-file 3D sandbox: gather blocks from the procedural world, pick up the spinning drops, and place the exact material selected in your hotbar.

Move WASD to walk, Space to jump. Arrow keys also move for browser automation tests.
Look & Use Click to lock the cursor, move the mouse to look, LMB to break, RMB to place.
Inventory Scroll or press 1-3 to choose grass, dirt, or stone. The bar shows your collected amounts.
World The terrain expands in chunks while you explore, blending open plains with rolling hills.
