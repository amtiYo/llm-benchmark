Voxel Sandbox
A complete browser voxel loop: gather blocks as drops, auto-pick them up, and build directly in the procedural world.
WASDMove around the world.
SpaceJump with collision against terrain and builds.
LMBMine the targeted block. It turns into a spinning drop with gravity.
RMBPlace exactly the selected block type from the hotbar.
Wheel / 1-5Select the active inventory slot.
