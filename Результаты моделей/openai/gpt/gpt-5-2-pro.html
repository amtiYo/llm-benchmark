Voxel Sandbox

Procedural voxel world (chunked), mine blocks (drops fall + spin), auto-pickup inventory, and build with the selected slot.

Controls: Click to capture mouse • WASD move • Space jump • Mouse look • LMB break • RMB place • 1/2/3 select • Wheel cycle • Esc unlock

Tip: Mine a few blocks, pick up the drops by walking near them, then place blocks with RMB.
