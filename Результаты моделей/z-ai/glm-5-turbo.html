Voxel Sandbox

procedural world
Click anywhere to play
WASD — Move  |  Space — Jump

``` This implements a complete voxel sandbox game with: **World Generation** - Procedural terrain using Perlin noise (FBM) with multiple octaves for natural-looking hills and plains - Chunks (16×48×16) load/unload dynamically as you move, creating an infinite-feeling world - Trees spawn on grass blocks with wood trunks and leaf canopies - Different biomes: grassy hills, sandy lowlands, stone beneath **Mining & Drops** - LMB breaks blocks via raycasting (step-based, checking ~140 points along the ray) - Broken blocks spawn as small spinning cubes that fly outward with initial velocity - Drops have full physics: gravity, ground collision, wall bouncing - Drops bob gently and rotate continuously - Walking within 1.8 units auto-picks up drops into inventory **Inventory & Building** - 9-slot hotbar with visual block previews and counts - Select slots with number keys 1-9 or mouse scroll wheel - Active slot highlighted with white border - Block name displayed above hotbar - RMB places the selected block type at the targeted face - Prevents placing blocks inside the player's collision box **Controls & Camera** - Pointer lock first-person view with smooth mouse look - WASD movement with collision detection (can't walk through walls) - Space to jump with proper gravity - Separate axis collision resolution (X, Y, Z) prevents corner-catching bugs - Wireframe highlight shows which block you're targeting
