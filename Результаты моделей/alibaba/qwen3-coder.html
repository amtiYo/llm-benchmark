BlockCraft Controls

  • WASD - Move around
  • Mouse - Look around
  • Space - Jump
  • Left Click - Break blocks
  • Right Click - Place blocks
  • 1-5 - Select inventory slots
  • E - Toggle inventory

BLOCKCRAFT

A 3D sandbox game where you mine, gather resources, and build your own world. Explore infinite terrain, collect different block types, and construct amazing structures!
