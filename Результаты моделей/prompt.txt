Write a complete browser-based 3D “sandbox” game inside a single HTML file.Main idea:Implement a core gameplay loop of gathering resources and building in a procedural world.Required gameplay mechanics:1) World & Generation- The world must be made of voxels (cubes) and generated in chunks as the player moves (infinite or expanding world).- The terrain must be procedural and include both hills and flat plains.2) Mining / Loot- When a block is destroyed, it must not simply disappear — it should drop as a small spinning item (“drop”) onto the ground with simple physics (gravity).- If the player gets close to the dropped item, it should be automatically picked up and added to the inventory.3) Inventory & Building- Implement an inventory UI (a slot bar on the screen).- The inventory must display the amount of collected blocks for each type.- The player can select an active slot and place exactly the block type selected.4) Controls & Camera- First-person view (hidden cursor, mouse-look camera control).- Movement: WASD + Jump, with collision (no walking through walls).- LMB — break blocks, RMB — place blocks.Focus:Prioritize reliable interaction between these mechanics as one connected, working gameplay loop.
